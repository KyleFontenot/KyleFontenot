2018

After the completion of ReviewMate, a diversification in aesthetic possibility was necessary. Striking while the fire was hot is exactly what was necessary for following the freelance path. Dream's is a complete, non-functioning website concept for the archival.

Dream's Ice Cream was a very fun website to create. After finishing ReviewMate's website, I wanted to further develop my craft by diversifying visual elements. In contrast to RevieMate's more formal, safer color palette, I wanted to experiment with negative space, minimal design, a "cute" feeling, and just general alterations to the typical T model that most websites are based on.

When creating ReviewMate, I used Bootstrap, so I wanted to experiment with other CSS frameworks as well. Dream's uses Bulma, a trending framework at the time that had clean characteristics to the visual styles. It would be perfect for the minimalistic style that I was going for, and most likely contributed ideas to my own CSS framework: Forten.
