Rustique

2020

What started as a vague website concept for a school assignment became a very usable consideration for another family business. A venture in new aesthetics, the Rustique website is an almost-ready implementation ready for review and consideration.

Rustique
CSS
HTML

This session-long project for one of my web-design classes at A.S.U. was to completely construct a site from the bottom up. The class was instructed to make up a business that would make this project believeable, but luckily for me, Rustique is actually my sister and her husband's business.

My sister and I had been discussing their internet presence for a long time since they don't have a website. This made assessing the user's and the stakeholder's needs very efficient. In addition to considering business needs, Rustique is a furniture business, so building upon an already physical aesthetic was perfect practice in web design.
