As I was studying basic web development avocationally in my early collegiate years, the perfect job had been offered to me. ReviewMate is my mother's proprietary software in the medical auditing industry. Resourcing work to internal "personnel" had been one of the highest priorities since inception, so naturally, I was requested to develop their customer-facing marketing website for them.

ReviewMate is a family business, but I am the independent contractor that helped creating a website. Initially, all software was attributed to a main technology major role, but stemming fron-end devleopment improved not only their custoimer-facing site, but logo refinement and social media platforms. This was my first experience into delving into logo creation and refinement, but seemingly simple standards like creating logos in a vector format was not for granted. The single most important aspect of the website that I would like to build upon is a contact form. At the time, learning PHP basics and the ins and outs of email servers seemed steep and out of scope for my current general education studies.

I plan to completely redesign the entire customer-facing platform by February 2021.
